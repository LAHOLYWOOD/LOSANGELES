Los Angeles in March

March in Los Angeles offers mild and pleasant weather,light rainfall with increasing daylight hours and the start of spring blooms.

The average temperature is 16.5 °C , with a maximum of 22°C and a minimum of 11°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 15°C or exceeding 30°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 8°C or exceeding 15°C.

The average sea temperature is 15°C.

Precipitation amounts to 57 mm, distributed over 5 days.

Sunlight in March at Los Angeles lasts for 12 hours on average. Average daylight hours are 9 hours.The sun shines 72 % of the time.

As March progresses, the days become longer, providing more daylight hours for outdoor activities. Daylight saving time usually begins in early March, which further extends the daylight hours. March is a popular time for spring break vacations, so some tourist areas and attractions may be busier during this period.
