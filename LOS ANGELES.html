LOS ANGELES CLIMATE

Los Angeles is the largest city in California and second largest city in US after New York.

The climate of Los Angeles is Mediterranean, characterized by very mild rainy winters and hot, sunny summers.

Dry season is from April to November while wet season is from December to March.

It has 266 days of sun.

The best time to visit Los Angeles is from May to October.

The clearest month of the year is August while the cloudiest month of the year is December.

Winter(December-March ) is generally mild and pleasant compared to many other parts of the United States.It is mostly clear skies. Winter is the rainy season in Los Angeles. You can expect periodic rainfall from December through March. The city relies on these winter rains to replenish its water supply and maintain green landscapes. Occasionally during the winter months, Los Angeles experiences Santa Ana winds, which are warm, dry winds that blow from the desert regions. These winds can lead to increased fire danger due to their drying effect on vegetation.

Spring(April-June) is a delightful season characterized by mild and pleasant weather, making it one of the most popular times to visit or live in the city. It is mistly clear skies. You'll find a variety of colorful flowers, including cherry blossoms, jacarandas, and wildflowers, adding beauty to the city's landscapes and parks. As spring progresses, the days become longer, providing more daylight hours for outdoor exploration and enjoyment.

Summer(July-September) typically warm and sunny characterized by warm to hot temperatures. It is clear skies. Daytime highs often range from the mid-70s to the mid-80s Fahrenheit (24-30 degrees Celsius) near the coast, while temperatures can rise into the 90s Fahrenheit (32-37 degrees Celsius) or even higher in inland and valley areas. Heatwaves can occur, particularly in late summer. The relative humidity levels are generally low in Los Angeles during the summer, which can make the warm temperatures feel more comfortable. Summer days are long in Los Angeles, with extended daylight hours providing plenty of time for outdoor adventures and exploration. Summer is ideal for outdoor activities like hiking, biking, surfing, and exploring the city's parks and recreational areas. Many outdoor concerts, movie nights, and festivals take place during this season.

Autumn(October-November) is a lovely and enjoyable season characterized by milder temperatures and changing foliage. It is mostly clear skies. As autumn progresses, the days become shorter, leading to less daylight. However, you can still enjoy outdoor activities during the comfortable daytime temperatures. The city embraces Halloween with decorations, haunted houses, and themed events. Thanksgiving is also a significant holiday celebrated with family gatherings and special meals.
