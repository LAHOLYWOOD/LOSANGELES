Los Angeles in December

December, the coldest month in Los Angeles, offers mild and cool weather, with occasional rainfall and shorter daylight hours.

The average temperature is 14°C , with a maximum of 20°C and a minimum of 8°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 13°C or exceeding 27°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 4°C or exceeding 15°C.

The average sea temperature is 16°C.

Precipitation amounts to 63 mm, distributed over 6 days.

December marks the beginning of the rainy season in Los Angeles. Be prepared for occasional showers and overcast days.

Sunlight in December at Los Angeles lasts for 10 hours on average. Average daylight hours are 7 hours.The sun shines 71 % of the time.

December features some of the shortest daylight hours of the year in Los Angeles, with limited daylight for outdoor activities.

Despite the cooler and wetter weather, December is still a suitable time for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and exploring the city's parks and natural attractions. Just be prepared for changing weather conditions.

Christmas and New Year's Eve are celebrated with enthusiasm in Los Angeles. You'll find special events, holiday markets, and fireworks displays to ring in the new year.

December is a festive month in Los Angeles, with various holiday celebrations and events taking place throughout the city, including tree lighting ceremonies and holiday parades.
