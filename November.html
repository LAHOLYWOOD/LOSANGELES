Los Angeles in November

November in Los Angeles offers mild and comfortable fall-like weather, with the possibility of some overcast days

The average temperature is 17°C , with a maximum of 23°C and a minimum of 11°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 17°C or exceeding 32°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 8°C or exceeding 17°C.

The average sea temperature is 18°C.

Precipitation amounts to 20 mm, distributed over 3 days.

Rainfall remains relatively infrequent in November, the region offers dry weather in Los Angeles.

Sunlight in November at Los Angeles lasts for 10 hours and 30 minutes on average. Average daylight hours are 7 hours.The sun shines 69 % of the time.

As November progresses, the days become noticeably shorter, with less daylight for outdoor activities.

November is still a suitable time for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, picnicking, and exploring the city's parks and natural attractions. The comfortable temperatures make it enjoyable for outdoor excursions.

November includes the Thanksgiving holiday, which is celebrated with family gatherings and special meals throughout the city.

In Los Angeles, daylight saving time (DST) typically ends on the first Sunday in November. On this day, clocks are set back one hour at 2:00 AM local time. This transition marks the return to standard time.
