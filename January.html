Los Angeles in January

January offers mild and comfortable weather, with occasional light rainfall.

The average temperature is 14.5 °C , with a maximum of 20°C and a minimum of 9°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 14°C or exceeding 28°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 5°C or exceeding 14°C.

The average sea temperature is 14°C.

Precipitation amounts to 84 mm, distributed over 6 days.

Sunlight in January at Los Angeles lasts for 10 hours on average. Average daylight hours are 7 hours.The sun shines 73 % of the time.

January is a great time for outdoor activities in Los Angeles. With mild temperatures and less crowded attractions, you can explore the city's parks, hike in the nearby mountains, or visit the beach.
