Los Angeles in October

October provides a warm and enjoyable climate in Los Angeles as the city transitions from late summer to early fall.

The average temperature is 21°C , with a maximum of 26°C and a minimum of 16°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 21°C or exceeding 35°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 12°C or exceeding 21°C.

The average sea temperature is 19°C.

Precipitation amounts to 15 mm, distributed over 2 days.

Rainfall remains rare in October, and the region generally experiences dry and rain-free weather throughout the month.

Sunlight in October at Los Angeles lasts for 11 hours and 30 minutes on average. Average daylight hours are 8 hours.The sun shines 72 % of the time.

While the days are still relatively long in October, you'll notice a gradual shortening of daylight hours as the city moves toward winter.

October is an ideal time for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, picnicking, and exploring the city's parks and natural attractions.

October marks the end of the peak tourist season in Los Angeles, so crowds at popular attractions and tourist spots begin to thin out.

Halloween in Los Angeles during October is a thrilling and festive time, with a wide range of spooky and fun activities for residents and visitors to enjoy.
