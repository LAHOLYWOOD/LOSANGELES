Los Angeles in April

April in Los Angeles is a good time, characterized by mild and pleasant weather as spring fully takes hold.

The average temperature is 17.5 °C , with a maximum of 23°C and a minimum of 12°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 16°C or exceeding 32°C.

Rainfall is rare in April, and the region experiences dry conditions.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 10°C or exceeding 16°C.

The average sea temperature is 16°C.

Precipitation amounts to 18 mm, distributed over 2 days.

Sunlight in April at Los Angeles lasts for 13 hours a day on average. Average daylight hours are 10 hours.The sun shines 78 % of the time.

With spring progressing, the days continue to get longer, providing ample daylight hours for outdoor activities. This is a great time for those who want to make the most of their time outdoors.
