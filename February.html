Los Angeles in February

February in Los Angeles offers mild and comfortable weather with the continuation of the rainy season, although rainfall remains relatively light.

The average temperature is of 15.5°C , with a maximum of 21 °C and a minimum of 10 °C .

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 14°C or exceeding 28°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 6°C or exceeding 14°C.

The average sea temperature is 14°C.

Precipitation amounts to 92 mm, distributed over 6 days.

Sunlight in February at Los Angeles lasts for 11 hours on average. Average daylight hours are 8 hours.The sun shines 71 % of the time.

February is a great time for outdoor activities in Los Angeles. With mild temperatures and fewer crowds at popular attractions, you can explore the city, visit parks, go hiking, or enjoy the beaches.
