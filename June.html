Los Angeles in June

June in Los Angeles offers warm, sunny, and dry weather, making it an excellent time for outdoor activities, beach visits, and enjoying the city's outdoor attractions.

The average temperature is 21°C , with a maximum of 26°C and a minimum of 16°C

Daily high temperatures rarely falls below 21°C or exceeding 32°C.

Daily low temperatures rarely falls below 14°C or exceeding 20°C.

The average sea temperature is 18°C.

Precipitation amounts to 8 mm, distributed over 2 days.

Rainfall is rare in June, and the region experiences dry conditions. You can expect mostly sunny and rain-free days.

Sunlight in June at Los Angeles lasts for 14 hours and 30 minutes a day on average. Average daylight hours are 9 hours.The sun shines 63 % of the time.

June boasts some of the longest daylight hours of the year, providing plenty of time for outdoor adventures and exploration.
